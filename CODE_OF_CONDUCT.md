# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in Solidity Smart Audit a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Any other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and to ban contributors for behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
